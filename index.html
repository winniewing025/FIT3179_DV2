How Malaysia's Agricultural Heartland Fuels the Nation's Economy
An analysis of the relationship between agricultural production and economic growth across Malaysian states

Introduction

Agriculture is a key sector in Malaysia, but its contribution varies significantly across states. Some regions specialize in specific crops while others maintain balanced production. Understanding these patterns helps identify economic opportunities.

This analysis explores which states are agricultural powerhouses, what they grow, and how this relates to economic performance.
